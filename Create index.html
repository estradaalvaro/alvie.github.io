Leg Locks

Leglocks has been a huge part of the No-Gi Jiu Jitsu revolution in the past couple of years.
Its not something new to the sport, we've seen it being applied with great success in a world stage competition circuit by people like Dean Lister for instance, but it was popularized and reinvented by the infamous DDS with Gordon Ryan, Garry Tonon and Eddie Cummings.
They showed the world that leglocks are effective and anyone can learn it if understood and apply in a systematic way.

In this page, we'll display this system. It is strongly recommeneded to watch the video series in BJJ Fanatics system: sequential progression
